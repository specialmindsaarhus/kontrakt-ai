FRANCHISE AGREEMENT TEST DOCUMENT

This is a test franchise agreement for Acme Franchise Company.

1. TERRITORY: The franchisee shall operate in the territory of Copenhagen, Denmark.

2. FEES: Initial franchise fee is 50,000 DKK. Monthly royalty is 5% of gross revenue.

3. TERM: The agreement is valid for 5 years from the signing date.

4. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate with 90 days written notice.
